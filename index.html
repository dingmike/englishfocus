Privacy Policy

We highly value and protect your personal privacy. Built upon a no-login, local-first architecture, the app secures user data strictly in compliance with international privacy standards.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information (PII), telephone numbers, email addresses, or social accounts. Your learning behaviors remain fully anonymous.

2. Third-Party AI Service Data Declaration

When you select a complex sentence or a word for linguistic analysis, the app securely transmits only the selected raw English text via standard HTTPS to the compliant third-party AI provider (DeepSeek API). This process contains absolutely zero user identifiers or tracking codes, and the payload is utilized solely for instant grammar and vocabulary parsing.

3. Data Storage and iCloud Synchronization

Your bookmarked words and FSRS learning metrics are stored locally within your device's secure sandbox.
